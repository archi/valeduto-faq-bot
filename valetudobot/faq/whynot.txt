title: Why not Valetudo 
short-title: whynot

text:
Valetudo is opinionated software and does not aim for feature parity.
Also be reminded it replaces only the cloud, not the actual firmware/business logic!
To figure out why Valetudo might not be for you, check out https://valetudo.cloud/pages/general/why-not-valetudo.html
