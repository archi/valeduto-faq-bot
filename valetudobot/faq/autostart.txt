keywords: autostart
title: Setup Valetudo to autostart
short-title: autostart-valetudo
text:
For DREAME robots:
1. download the latest binary for your model of robot (as per "/faq manual")
2. chmod +x /data/valetudo
4. cp /misc/_root_postboot.sh.tpl /data/_root_postboot.sh
5. chmod +x /data/_root_postboot.sh
6. reboot
