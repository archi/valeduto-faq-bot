keywords: autostart
title: Setup Valetudo to autostart
short-title: autostart-valetudo
text:
For DREAME robots:
1. download the latest binary for your model of robot (as per <code>/faq update</code>)
2. <code>chmod +x /data/valetudo</code>
3. <code>cp /misc/_root_postboot.sh.tpl /data/_root_postboot.sh</code>
4. <code>chmod +x /data/_root_postboot.sh</code>
5. <code>reboot</code>
