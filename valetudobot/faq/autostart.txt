title: Setup Valetudo to autostart
short-title: autostart-valetudo
text:
For DREAME robots:
1. download the latest binary for your model of robot (as per <pre>/faq manual</pre>)
2. <pre>chmod +x /data/valetudo</pre>
4. <pre>cp /misc/_root_postboot.sh.tpl /data/_root_postboot.sh</pre>
5. <pre>chmod +x /data/_root_postboot.sh</pre>
6. <pre>reboot</pre>
